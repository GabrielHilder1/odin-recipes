Spaghetti Bolognaise

Description

A simple recipe that anyone can make

This recipe requires few ingredients and can be made in a relatively short amount of time

Ingredients

  • spaghetti pasta
  • tomato pasta sauce
  • grated cheese
  • 500g beef mince
  • olive oil
    •

    Steps

    1. Cook the pasta in boiling water until al dente
    2. Brown the mince in the oil in a cooking pan
    3. Add the pasta sauce to the mince in the pan and reduce heat to a simmer
    4. Simmer ingredients until sauce thickens
    5. Once sauce has thickened it is ready to serve
    6. Add grated cheese on top and eat
